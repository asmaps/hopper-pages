hopper.pw

Shutdown notice

Due to repeated abuse complaints and thus service disruptions I decided to not longer run this service.

Dealing with these cases takes a lot of time and requires immediate action. As hopper.pw was run as a spare time project I cannot guarantee this and do not have the time to constantly monitor and prevent abuse.

Where can I host my dynamic DNS now?

An alternative to hopper.pw is nsupdate.info it is also open source and non-commercial.

For more alternatives check Google™ or services like AlternativeTo

Thanks to all supporters.
I hope you understand this decision and find another service.
And to all abusers: Shame on you!
